A timeline of the Hymenopteran fossil record

Alex Pinch, October 2025

Hymenoptera are an order of insects including bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies. Here I outline what we have gleaned from a somewhat complex fossil record, and where this agrees with phylogenomic studies of extant taxa. Taxonomic studies have traditionally placed sawflies as a basal paraphyletic suborder called Symphyta, which excludes the suborder Apocrita
